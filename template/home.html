This is an webapp that allows you to create an imagelist. The image should be in jpg or in png format that can be uploaded by clicking on the following Add Image button. The image list can be viewed by clicking on Image View button. The Image list will only show you the thumbnail of the image uploaded. If you want to see the original image just click on the thumbnail of the image. Your original image will be shown in another web-page.
